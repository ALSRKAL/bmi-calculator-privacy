# Privacy Policy for BMI Calculator

**Last Updated:** December 2, 2024

## Introduction

BMI Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Personal Health Data**: Age, height, weight, and gender that you enter to calculate your BMI
- **Note**: All this data is stored **locally on your device only** and is never transmitted to our servers

### Information Collected Automatically
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising ID
- IP address
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- App usage data
- Location data (approximate, based on IP address)

## How We Use Information

### Personal Health Data
- Stored locally on your device only
- Used solely to calculate your BMI and display results
- Never shared with third parties
- Never transmitted to our servers
- You can clear this data by uninstalling the app

### Advertising Data (Collected by AdMob)
- To display personalized advertisements
- To improve ad performance and relevance
- To analyze app usage patterns

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is a third-party service that may collect information as described in their privacy policy:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google AdMob Privacy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to:
- Serve personalized ads based on your interests
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud

### Opting Out of Personalized Ads
You can opt out of personalized advertising by:
- **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Your BMI calculations are stored locally using SharedPreferences (Android) or UserDefaults (iOS)
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store any of your personal data on our servers
- **Security**: While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View the data stored locally on your device
- **Delete**: Clear all data by uninstalling the app
- **Opt-Out**: Disable personalized ads through your device settings

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- **AdMob Data**: Retained according to Google's data retention policies

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
