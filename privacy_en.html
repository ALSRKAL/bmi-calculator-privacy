Introduction

BMI Calculator is a simple application for calculating Body Mass Index. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Store

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Age, height, weight, and gender you enter for BMI calculation
  • BMI calculation history (up to 50 entries)
  • App settings (language preference, theme mode)

Important: All this data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
